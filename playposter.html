Icon Inspired Poster

I've always thought posters are an ideal canvas for implementing several design elements and principles in the same design. My motivation with every poster is to do that as much as possible without taking away from the information on the poster. Instead, I want to use typographic heirarchy, for example, to my advantage in displaying this type of information. This poster is an example of how the heirarchy and negative space worked together to make it cohesive.
