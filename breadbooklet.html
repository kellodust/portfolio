The Living Bread Booklet

This project consisted of creating a gift booklet with someone in mind that included a talk given in the last General Conference of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The booklet had to include folios with page numbers, a front and back cover, a photo and caption of the speaker as well as the full talk.

Adding all the details

I took the liberty of including as well a table of contents, a section for notes, a personal letter to the recipient and references throughout the talk. This booklet focuses on the talk given by Elder D. Todd Christofferson, "The Living Bread Which Came Down From Heaven."

Matching the design to the content

You will find a stair-step theme in the text—this is symbolic of the title of the talk. I wanted to emphasize the symbolism in the content because the talk was religously based. I also felt a need to simplify the design to only typographic work so that the content could shine through with only subtle symbolisms that illustrate the meaning of the talk.

Knowing the Audience

As this project was meant to be a gift, I felt that the most important thing was making sure the recipient loved it. Knowing them well and knowing how organized they are, I knew that they would appreciate those little extra efforts like a page for notes and table of contents. I also knew that, as a fellow deep thinker, they would appreciate the subtle symbolism and the emphasis on the content.
