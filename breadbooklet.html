The Living Bread Booklet

This project consisted of creating a gift booklet with someone in mind that included a talk given in the last General Conference of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The booklet had to include folios with page numbers, a front and back cover, a photo and caption of the speaker as well as the full talk. I took the liberty of including as well a table of contents, a section for notes, a personal letter to the recipient and references throughout the talk. This booklet focuses on the talk given by Elder D. Todd Christofferson, "The Living Bread Which Came Down From Heaven."
