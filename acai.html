Information Design

Acaí: An Infographic

This passion project features an infographic about acaí and the origin thereof. It features the information about the Amazon and the importance of the acaí berry to the Amazon Rainforest.

Having Passion for the Work

The infographic was particularly special to me because of the passion I had for the information in it. I've spent a year and a half in the Amazon Rainforest so when I started the project I wanted to give the design and the research the time it deserved. The Amazon people have a very rich culture and are curiously obsessed with the acaí berry. The berry was the perfect subject for my infographic as I truly felt that what I was able to communicate in the infographic would help others to understand their love and need for the berry.
